The Game & Watch, originally introduced by Nintendo in 1980, marked a significant milestone in portable gaming history with its pocket-sized LCD screen and simple, single-game functionality. It paved the way for Nintendo's future innovations and ultimately led to the development of the Game Boy and the modern handheld gaming industry.
