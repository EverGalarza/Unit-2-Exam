The Nintendo GameCube, released in 2001, was a beloved gaming console known for its compact design and a strong library of iconic games like Super Smash Bros. Melee and The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker. Its unique mini-disc format and innovative controller design contributed to its distinctive charm in the world of gaming.
